Hey,

I’m Joann Heng Rarumangkay, but feel free to call me Joann. a Visual Communication Design student with a passion for creating art that speaks with simplicity and sophistication. I love diving into projects where I get to make things look simple, but with all the subtle, polished details that bring it to life. My aesthetic is rooted in clean, graphic elements that bring a sense of balance and clarity to my work. Each project is an opportunity to blend functionality with beauty of creating pieces that feel both timeless and contemporary.

Projects

The projects I have worked on so far are based on assignments and projects during college activities.

STOP FOOD WASTE

Through a thoughtful combination of visuals and messaging, the poster aims to raise awareness about the critical issue of food waste and inspire viewers to take responsible actions.

RORO JONGGRANG

In this work, I experimented with screen printing on clothing, inspired by the Roro Jonggrang folklore. The visual background features Prambanan Temple, reflecting the story's setting.

METAPHORE POSTER

The visual elements are meticulously crafted to convey a strong message against gender-based violence, urging society to stop the perpetuation of such harmful behaviors.

PANGOLINS

I created an event booklet that tells the story of the pangolin theme, aiming to inform attendees about the purpose and details of the event.

PIECES OF ME

I interpret my works results in a portfolio booklet with a puzzle pieces theme, symbolizing each individual piece as part of my overall collection of works and life.

HARMONY

My theme focuses on sustainability using fabric scraps, developed into a poster titled "HARMONY." This concept reflects the diversity, beauty, and cultural richness of Indonesia through the unique patterns and colors of the fabric scraps.

GENTLE MONSTER

A Photography project that using fashion items as a theme. I decided to use sunglasses from Gentle Monster, Korea.

PHANTOM QUEEN

This game follows Nyx, a "shadow girl," on a mission to steal a diamond from an unjust palace. The focus is on helping the people by reclaiming palace wealth, overcoming obstacles, and completing her mission successfully.

CATCHA!

The 3D game poster featuring the iconic Claw Machine.

HAPPY SOJU

Project of creative advertisement poster for happy soju. This captivating artwork seamlessly blends sophistication and flavor, inviting consumers to indulge in a unique drinking experience.

BARBIE MOVIE POSTER

The enchanting world of the iconic Barbie that takes a culturally immersive journey through the vibrant landscapes of Bali. This movie poster is a visual feast that seamlessly merges Barbie's timeless allure with the rich.

STARRY GLEAM

I developed this theme into a poster titled "STARRY GLEAM," symbolizing moments of happiness from simple, meaningful things. Fabric scraps represent a "rain of joy," where small, overlooked pieces unite into something beautiful.

A CHANCE FOR CHANGE

Explore our "Tree Aid" brochure, a journey into environmental stewardship, promoting ecological awareness and sustainable action.

PAVING A PATH LOGOGRAM

This visual explores the theme of how each individual creates a profound new understanding when experiencing art.

TUMBLER COFFEE

For the rebranding design of this small business coffee shop, I chose a bamboo- inspired theme to reflect values of communication, relaxation, and quality.

ART GALLERY PACKAGING

I chose an art gallery theme as a rebranding design of bakery, symbolizing a journey full of flavors in every "painting" of bread.

SIGHT FROM THE EARTH

Eco-fashion brand that is rebranded with the theme "Geometry of Life," emphasizing the interconnectedness and balance found in nature through geometric design elements.

TREE AID LOGO

The rebranding of the Tree Aid logo, a community focused on growing trees to tackle poverty in Africa.
